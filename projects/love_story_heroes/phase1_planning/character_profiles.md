# Character Profiles

## Male Protagonist: Elias Thorne

### Basic Information
-   **Name**: Elias Thorne
-   **Age**: 28
-   **Occupation**: Author of "Speculative Fiction" (Guardian of Earth)
-   **Appearance**:
    -   Messy dark hair that looks like he's been pulling at it.
    -   Ink stains constantly on his fingers.
    -   Weary eyes with dark circles, but they light up when he has an idea.
    -   Always wears comfortable, worn-out cardigans.

### Personality
-   **Anxious Protector**: Carries the weight of the world; worries constantly about "bad endings."
-   **Chaotic Creative**: Brilliant imagination but lacks discipline; his magic is wild and untamed.
-   **Self-Sacrificial**: Writes tragedy for himself so others can have comedies.
-   **Gentle**: Despite fighting monsters, he hates violence. He defeats them by "writing them away" or "redeeming" them.

### Background Story
Discovered his ability at 15 when he wrote a story about his bully vanishing, and the bully actually moved to another city the next day. Recruited by the "Librarians" (a secret order) to hold back the narrative entropy. He has been lonely for a decade, afraid to get close to anyone lest they become a "character" in his dangerous stories.

### Internal Struggles
-   **Fear of Influence**: Afraid that if he loves someone, he will accidentally write them into tragedy.
-   **Burnout**: He is tired of being the only writer.

### Character Arc
-   **Beginning**: A solitary, exhausted god-figure drowning in chaos.
-   **Transformation**: Learning to share the pen. Accepting structure/help.
-   **End**: A partner. He is no longer writing alone.

## Female Protagonist: Maya Lin

### Basic Information
-   **Name**: Maya Lin
-   **Age**: 27
-   **Occupation**: Senior Editor at Chimera Publishing.
-   **Appearance**:
    -   Sharp bob cut, pristine professional attire.
    -   Piercing gaze (looks at people like she's checking for typos).
    -   Carries a red fountain pen everywhere.

### Personality
-   **Structured Realist**: Believes everything has a logic and a place. Dislikes chaos.
-   **Nurturing via Correction**: She "fixes" things because she cares. Editing is her love language.
-   **Secretly Romantic**: Loves stories, which is why she demands they be good.
-   **Brave**: Not physically strong, but mentally unshakable. Doesn't panic in the face of the impossible; she analyzes it.

### Background Story
Rose quickly in the publishing world due to her ruthless efficiency. She was assigned to Elias as a "punishment" because he's notoriously difficult/flakey. She sees it as a challenge to whip him into shape.
